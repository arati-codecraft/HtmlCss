Q4 Create a paragraph with subscript and superscript examples.

What is Subscript (sub) Subscript means small text written below the normal line of text. In HTML, we use the sub tag to write subscript text. It is often used in chemical formulas, mathematical notations, or footnotes.

What is Superscript (sup) Superscript means small text written above the normal line of text. In HTML, we use the sup tag to write superscript text. It is often used in mathematics (powers/exponents), ordinals, and scientific notations.

Waters chemical formula is H2O.

Carbon dioxide can be written as CO2, and Einstein’s equation is E = mc2.

Kalam studied physics and aerospace engineering. After graduating from the Madras Institute of Technology, he joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and later the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). He played a key role in developing India’s first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III), which deployed the Rohini satellite in orbit.

Known as the “Missile Man of India,” Dr. Kalam was instrumental in the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology. He also served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007, earning immense respect for his simplicity, integrity, and dedication to education and youth.

After his presidency, Dr. Kalam continued to teach and inspire students across India. He believed that the nation’s future lies in the hands of its youth and encouraged them to dream big and work hard. His famous quote, “Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action,” continues to motivate millions.

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam passed away on July 27, 2015, while delivering a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management, Shillong. His life remains a shining example of hard work, humility, and devotion to the nation.

Q9 Use blockquote for a long quotation.

The blockquote tag is used for long quotations that come from another source. It indents the quoted text by default to make it stand out. You can also include the cite attribute to specify the source (URL or book, etc.).

example

Q10 Create inline quotation using q.

The q tag is used for short, inline quotations — usually just a few words or a sentence. Browsers automatically add quotation marks (“ ”) around the text inside q tag. It’s used within a paragraph, unlike blockquote tag which is for long, separate quotes.

example

Albert Einstein also remarked, Imagination is more important than knowledge. It reminds us that creativity drives discovery and progress.

Q11 Show difference between b tag and strong tag.

b tag stands for bold. It makes the text bold in appearance, but does not add importance or meaning. It’s used only for styling, not for emphasizing importance.
strong tag is used to indicate that text is important or serious. Browsers also display it in bold, but it carries semantic meaning (used by search engines and screen readers).
